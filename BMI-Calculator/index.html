About BMI
BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²)
BMI is a general health indicator — it does not account for muscle mass or body composition.
Supports both Metric (kg/cm) and Imperial (lb/ft) units.
Consult a doctor for a complete health assessment.
