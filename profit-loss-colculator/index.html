Formulas Used
Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price (when SP > CP)
Loss = Cost Price − Selling Price (when CP > SP)
Profit % = (Profit ÷ Cost Price) × 100
Loss % = (Loss ÷ Cost Price) × 100
Margin % = (Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100
